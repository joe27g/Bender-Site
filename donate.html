PayPal
Make a one-time contribution to Bender's creator to help develop the bot.
For $1 or more, you will receive the @Donor role, and will have access to a channel to chat with other donors and staff!
For $10 or more, you will receive the @Supporter role plus Donor benefits.
For $25 or more, you will recieve the @Sponsor role as well as your name mentioned on the homepage as a thank-you. You may also request a custom role on Discord!
Patreon
Make a monthly contribution to provide continuous support and keep Bender running smoothly!
For $1/month or more, you will receive the @Donor role, and will have access to a channel to chat with other donors and staff!
For $5/month or more, you will receive the @Supporter role plus Donor benefits.
For $10/month or more, you will recieve the @Sponsor role as well as your name mentioned on the homepage as a thank-you. You may also request a custom role on Discord!
Bender Pro
Buying Bender Pro is an alternative to donating. Not only will you contribute to keeping Bender up and running, you will also get access to several exclusive features!
For more info, see the Bender Pro info page.
