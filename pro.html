Bender Pro enables lots of new features, including:

  • - Image manipulation commands: ;blur, ;brightness, ;contrast, ;deepfry, ;graycale, ;invert, ;jpeg, ;mirror, ;pulse, ;rotate, ;saturate, ;sepia, ;shake, and ;spin
  • - Meme creation commands: ;bobross, ;brain, ;changemymind, ;gay, ;gaysingles, ;killit, ;steppedinshit, and more.
  • - ;news ~ Show news for a game, or configure it to automatically be posted in a certain channel.
  • - You can have up to 7 autoroles rather than just 1.
  • - ;roleall ~ Add, remove, or toggle roles for all server members at once.
  • - ;nickall ~ Set or reset nicknames of all server members.
  • - ;volume ~ Change volume at which Bender plays music.
  • - ;autoplay ~ Enable related songs to play after the queue ends.
  • - You can play songs up to 24 hours instead of just 2 hours.
  • - Save playlists that you can play on any server with Bender.

More Bender Pro features are in development, and we're open to suggestions on what to add.
If you have an idea for a new feature, feel free to type it in #suggestions in Bender's Lair.

After purchasing and linking your Discord account on the dashboard, you will also receive the @Bender Pro role in Bender's Lair.
